Overview

This is the working outline and beginning scripts for Noah: The Culling graphic novel. Character bios and location notes are also available as part of the production notebook. Of course, everything you see here is subject to change.
Be warned! This is the script for the book and you're wellcome to read, but it'll naturally spoil the story.
  • Volume I: The Corruption Noah and his family are no longer able to lead their lives apart, and are now targetted by the fallen folk. Visions of the flood begin.
  • Volume II: The Submission Noah accepts the visions and moves his family deep into the forest while. He gathers his sons and tells them of the great quests ahead.
  • Volume III: The Quest Part I We follow Ham and Japheth as they journey into impenetrable jungle, teaming with life, and snow capped mountains where lean creatures grip fiercly to life.
  • Volume IV: The Quest Part II Noah travels to the plains to treat with the King of Beasts. Meanwhile, Japeth must brave terrifying seas to reach the Leviathan.
  • Volume V: The Ark Meanwhile, Set, Nora, Sare, and Serac (the wives) work with the beasts of man and the few followers drawn to Noah to build the Ark amidst growing danger.
  • Volume VI: The Flood The path of events is now clear, and the great gathering of creatures cannot help but draw the fallen folk and their terrible lizards into one last, great battle.
  • Volume VII: The Covenant Forty days and forty nights the Ark is tossed and rolls and God's destruction is complete. Then, there is the dawning of the first new day and Noah is given a final vision of God's new covenant with man.
