'Father, our enemies breach the western defenses.'
'Then let us prepare a greeting.'
Tight shot. Japheth and Noah gaze over a gunwale. Japeth stretches his arm towards a mass of dinos pushing felled trees into the ditch ringing the ark.

'Noah has laid up fine provisions and built a magnificent slaughterhouse to boot. I wager there's meat enough for a fortnight of feasting. Come, let us test his hospitality.'
Medium shot. Abaddon rides a styracosaurus in front of a mass of humans and assorted dinos. The humans brandish crude weapons, the dinos roar. Abaddon holds reins in one hand, and raises his fish aloft.
