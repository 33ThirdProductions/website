Noble, courageous, and wise, Japheth is Noah's most trusted advisor. Japheth believes he can best serve Yahweh and his family through the application of his mind and has dedicated himself to understanding God's creation.
Japheth sees the corrupted world as a great test, the sieve of humanity. Though superficially untroubled, his growing indifference to the world threatens to create a gulf between Japheth and his humanity; a void which threatens to engulf the self.
